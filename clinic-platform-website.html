Comprehensive Platform Features

Everything you need to connect, engage, and manage healthcare communications in one powerful platform

Smart Booking System

Patients can easily book appointments with clinics. Advanced confirmation system ensures privacy and security with detailed appointment management.

Live Streaming

Real-time live streaming powered by SignalR for promotions, health talks, and patient engagement. Interactive features for enhanced communication.

Role-Based Access

Comprehensive user management with JWT authentication. Separate interfaces and permissions for clinics and patients with enhanced security.

Reviews & Ratings

Patient feedback system allowing reviews and ratings for clinics. Build trust and transparency in healthcare services.

Smart Advertising

Advanced advertisement ranking algorithm based on clinic payments. Targeted promotion system for maximum visibility and engagement.

Email Notifications

Automated email alerts for bookings, confirmations, and important updates. Keep all stakeholders informed with timely notifications.
